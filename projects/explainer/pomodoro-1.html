Taking regular, short breaks gives the brain time to rest and recover after focusing for long periods of time. Doing this will help you avoid burnout and improve your attention span when you get back to work.







Try the Pomodoro Technique:

  1. Focus for 25 minutes.
  2. Take a 5-minute break.
  3. Repeat the above steps four times.
  4. Take a 15-30 minute break.
